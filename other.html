2023 MSc Thesis

Anti-plane analysis of the Francfort–Marigo model for quasi-static brittle fracture

Luca Pignatelli

MSc in Engineering Mathematics — Polytechnic University of Turin
Supervisor: Prof. Marco Morandotti

Analysis of the Francfort–Marigo variational model for brittle fracture in the anti-plane setting. The thesis studies existence of quasi-static evolutions, the role of Griffith's criterion, and properties of the crack path.

2021 BSc Thesis in Italian

Dimensione frattale e misura di Hausdorff

Luca Pignatelli

BSc in Mathematics for Engineering — Polytechnic University of Turin
Supervisor: Prof. Luca Lussardi

An introduction to fractal dimension and Hausdorff measure, including self-similar sets, the Moran–Hutchinson theorem, and classical examples such as the Cantor set and Koch curve.
